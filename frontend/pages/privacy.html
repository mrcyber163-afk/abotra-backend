Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Read how we protect your data.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use ABOTRA-PROAI, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, username, country of residence.
  • Financial Information: Deposit/withdrawal history, transaction records, trading volume, wallet addresses.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, browser information, operating system, and activity logs.
  • Verification Data: Government ID, passport, driver's license, proof of address, and selfie photos (for KYC compliance).

2. How We Use Your Information

ABOTRA-PROAI uses your data for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To create and manage your trading account
  • To process deposits, withdrawals, and P2P transactions
  • To calculate and credit robot profits to your balance
  • To prevent fraud, money laundering, and suspicious activities
  • To comply with international financial regulations and KYC requirements
  • To improve platform features, AI algorithms, and user experience
  • To send important notifications about account activity, security alerts, and platform updates
  • To respond to customer support requests and resolve disputes

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers with enterprise-grade encryption. We implement:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest using AES-256.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel with strict need-to-know access can view user data.
  • Firebase Security Rules: Database rules ensure users can only access their own information.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security audits and vulnerability scans every 30 days.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

ABOTRA-PROAI NEVER sells your personal data to third parties. However, we may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Fraud Prevention: With law enforcement agencies investigating fraudulent activities.
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate (payment processors, hosting services) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice to affected users.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and understand user behavior. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality. Third-party analytics (Google Analytics) may also collect anonymized usage data.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion and data removal (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data in certain situations.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@abotra-proai.com with your request.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
