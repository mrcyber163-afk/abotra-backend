Manual Trading Tutorial
Learn how to trade cryptocurrencies like a professional
Step-by-Step Trading Guide
1
📊 Understand Market Analysis
Before placing any trade, you need to analyze the market. There are two main types of analysis:
Technical Analysis
Using charts, indicators (RSI, MACD, Moving Averages) and price patterns to predict future movements.
Fundamental Analysis
News, economic events, market sentiment, and overall crypto adoption trends.
2
💰 Decide Your Position
Choose between BUY (Long) or SELL (Short) based on your market prediction.
BUY / LONG
You think the price will go UP. Example: BTC at $60,000 → you predict it will rise to $65,000.
SELL / SHORT
You think the price will go DOWN. Example: BTC at $60,000 → you predict it will drop to $55,000.
3
⚡ Set Your Trade Amount & Leverage
Decide how much capital to risk on this trade. Leverage multiplies your exposure.
Example Calculation
Trade Amount: $100 | Leverage: 10x = Position Size: $1,000
💡 Higher leverage = Higher potential profit BUT also higher risk!
⚠️ Recommended for beginners: 1x-5x leverage only.
4
🎯 Set Take Profit (TP) & Stop Loss (SL)
Protect your profits and limit your losses automatically.
Take Profit (TP)
Automatically closes your trade when price reaches your target profit.
Example: Buy at $60,000 → TP at $61,000 = $1,000 profit per BTC.
Stop Loss (SL)
Automatically closes your trade to prevent further losses.
Example: Buy at $60,000 → SL at $59,000 = $1,000 max loss per BTC.
5
🚀 Execute Your Trade
Once everything is set, click OPEN TRADE to enter the market.
After Opening
Your position will appear under "Open Positions" with real-time Profit/Loss (PnL).
You can watch it update as the price moves! 📈📉
6
📈 Monitor & Close Trade
Watch your open positions and close them manually when satisfied.
When to Close?
✓ Take profit when target reached
✓ Cut losses quickly using stop loss
✓ Close manually anytime by clicking "CLOSE TRADE"
💡 Pro Trading Tips
  • Never risk more than 1-5% of your total balance per trade
  • Always use Stop Loss - it's your safety net!
  • Start with small amounts while learning
  • Don't trade based on emotions (fear/greed)
  • Keep a trading journal to track your performance
  • High leverage (50x-100x) is for experts only!
Trading Glossary
📈 Bull Market
Prices are rising overall. Optimistic market sentiment.
📉 Bear Market
Prices are falling overall. Pessimistic market sentiment.
📊 PnL
Profit and Loss - your total gain or loss on open/closed trades.
⚡ Leverage
Borrowed capital to increase position size (2x to 100x).
🎯 Liquidation
When losses exceed your margin - position forced closed.
💧 Liquidity
How easily an asset can be bought/sold without affecting price.
