How AI Robots Work

  • FREE TRIAL BOT: 5 days, 10% daily profit. After 5 days, 50% profit to balance, 50% invested capital moves to Starter Bot.
  • Purchase Once, Own Forever — Each robot is a permanent purchase. No subscription fees.
  • Two Costs: Bot Price (to buy the robot) + Investment Amount (trading capital). You need BOTH to start trading.
  • Auto-Start on Purchase — After buying the robot, you must invest to activate trading. The robot starts immediately after investment.
  • After 120 days (4 months), your robot upgrades automatically to the next level (except Legendary). Your invested capital moves to the new robot.
  • LEGENDARY BOT: After 120 days, you pay bot price again to renew. Investment stays the same.
  • Each robot generates daily returns based on its level. Higher-level robots offer higher daily profits but require larger investment.
  • You can own multiple robots and run them simultaneously to diversify your portfolio.
  • After purchase, visit the "AI Bot" page to monitor your active robots and see daily earnings.
