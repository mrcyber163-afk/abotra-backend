How AI Robots Work

  • Purchase Once, Own Forever — Each robot is a permanent purchase. No subscription fees, no hidden costs. Buy it once, and it's yours forever.
  • Each robot purchase includes automatic trading capital — half of your payment goes to the robot, and half is invested in trading automatically.
  • We recommend setting your robot to 30 days when activating it. This allows the AI to optimize trading strategies over time and reduces the need to manually restart your bot every day.
  • After purchase, go to the "AI Bot" page to activate your robot, set your trading percentage, and choose the duration (1-30 days).
  • Each robot generates daily returns based on its level. Higher-level robots offer higher daily profits but require a larger initial investment.
  • You can own multiple robots and run them simultaneously to diversify your trading portfolio and maximize your daily earnings.
  • If you need help, visit the "AI Tutorial" page for a step-by-step guide on setting up and managing your AI trading robots.
