How AI Auto Trading Works

  • When you start a robot, 100% of your balance is locked for 365 days.
  • Daily profits are calculated based on your robot's daily percentage and credited to your balance every 24 hours.
  • Your locked capital (principal) is released after 365 days along with the final day's profit.
  • Important: Daily returns are approximations and NOT guaranteed. Actual returns depend on market conditions, AI performance, and trading strategies.
  • Higher level robots have higher daily profit percentages but also higher investment requirements.
  • Once started, the robot runs automatically for 365 days. You cannot withdraw the locked capital until the 365 days are complete.
  • You can add more capital to an active robot to increase potential earnings.
  • All trading is automated by AI - no manual intervention needed after starting.
  • After 365 days, you can manually restart the robot using the "Restart Robot" button.
