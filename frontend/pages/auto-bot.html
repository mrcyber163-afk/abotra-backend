How AI Auto Trading Works

  • When you start a robot, your investment is locked for 120 days (4 months).
  • Daily profits are calculated based on your robot's daily percentage and credited to your balance every 24 hours.
  • Your invested capital (principal) remains locked and after 120 days, the robot automatically upgrades to the next level.
  • After 120 days, you must buy the next level robot (bot price only) to continue trading. The invested capital moves to the new robot.
  • Important: Daily returns are approximations and NOT guaranteed. Actual returns depend on market conditions.
  • Higher level robots have higher daily profit percentages but also higher investment requirements.
  • You can add more capital to an active robot at any time to increase potential earnings.
  • All trading is automated by AI - no manual intervention needed after starting.
